Cloud Services

Organizations are embracing cloud to benefit from the scalability, responsiveness and cost-efficiencies that cloud computing offers. The transformation to a cloud environment is a significant enterprise undertaking that has a tremendous impact on the way IT organizations operate and the way services are delivered to business users.

We view cloud as a paradigm that enables organizations to operate more efficiently, while also further enables a service-based IT delivery model, resulting in improved responsiveness to business needs.

Our services include

Cloud Strategy and Consulting - Let Teknest guide you in identifying business and IT drivers for cloud strategies, defining a cloud roadmap, and selecting the right cloud platform.

Building Cloud Applications - We can help you transform your applications by hosting, extending or building them on IaaS or PaaS cloud platform.

Cloud Management - Discover the most efficient ways to provision and manage cloud resources.

Cloud Infrastructure - We can help you plan, build and manage a cloud infrastructure using your choice of innovative cloud services.
